All About Goodfellas

The story of the rise and fall of Henry Hill and his life in the mafia. A poor Irish-Italian growing up in 1950s New York City, rises through the ranks of his neighborhood's organized crime family. Through drugs and bad decisions Henry's life is in a downward spiral and he falls from favor with the mafia; leaving him to fight for his survival. After testifying against his former partners, Henry ends up in the FBI's witness protection program.

Production Company: Warner Bros.

Release Date: September 21, 1990

Genre: Biography, Crime, Drama

Rating: R

Run Time: 2 hours and 25 minutes
